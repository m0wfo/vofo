Building community

﻿﻿﻿A NYC-based 
organization focused 
on supporting victims and families of those impacted by a traumatic falling object.﻿﻿﻿
Accident survivors face a variety of different issues. While everyone has different challenges depending on the severity of their injury there are others undergoing similar challenges. Peers can best understand the problems you are facing.

The support group component of  Victims of Falling Objects (VOFO)  provides the emotional support survivors and their families need to tackle the obstacles of living with the accident. Support groups are a wonderful way to make new friends and to learn more about common injuries and how to help each other.
When we meet: 4th Thursday of the month

Time: 6:30-8pm

*Download to Outlook, iCalendar, gcal
What to bring:
- Yourself!
- Family members, friends or others you wish to share with
- A good sense of humor and positive attitude
- Willingness to be open and listen to others
